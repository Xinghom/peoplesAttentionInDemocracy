People’s attention to democracy

The purpose of the project is to show how people’s attention to democracy has changed over time from the year 1800 to 2000 in 5 different languages area. The covered keywords include democracy, justice, and liberty etc. The project measures people’s attention to the target topic by calculating the percentage of the times the target topic occurs in published books and documents in the year.
